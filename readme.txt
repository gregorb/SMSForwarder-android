Title: SMS Forwarder (free)
Short Description: Super simple no bells and whistles SMS forwarding application.
Full Description:


SMS Forwarder.

Forwards all your incoming SMS messages to another number. 
That's all.

For those of us that hate bloated apps that usually come with way too many features when we only need the app to do one simple thing. 
This app features: 
- no adds
- completely free
- no unnecessary contact or internet access permissions
- doesn't install any hidden services
- doesn't even have to be running - the system starts it when SMS arrives automatically

May or may not work with you phone or tablet. 
No guarantees.

Have fun!

---
Known to be working on: 
- Samsung Galaxy S4
- at least one cheap Chinese tablet
